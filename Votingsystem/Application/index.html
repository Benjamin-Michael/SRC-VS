      2021 SRC

      The CPUT student body is represented on the various decision making structures at the university by a Central Student Representative Council (CSRC) and four Local Student Representative Councils. Local Representative Councils are situated on the Bellville, Cape Town, Mowbray and Wellington Campuses.
